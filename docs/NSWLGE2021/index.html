2021 NSW local government election companion site

by Andrew Conway and Vanessa Teague

This is a companion website for our report on the possible effects of iVote on the 2021 NSW local government election.

You may see details on added or altered votes sufficient to have changed the outcome of the election. We are not making any claim about what did happen - we simply quantify the size of sufficient changes, using the data made available by NSWEC.
