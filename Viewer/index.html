Analysis of Election Data

by Andrew Conway and Vanessa Teague

This site gives background details for our analysis of election outcomes. You can generally reproduce these results yourself using the programs in the git repository https://github.com/AndrewConway/ConcreteSTV or just quickly look at the results here.

2020 ACT election

In our report we show three classes of bugs that could in principle change election outcomes in the ACT vote counting code that cropped up in the 2020 election. Elections ACT acknowledged two of the bug classes, and fixed all three and recounted the election in 2021.

Federal Senate elections 2013-2019

In our report we show one clear class of bug and two bizarre changes in count algorithm between the 2013, 2016 and 2019 elections where at least one of them was wrong. The end result was changing the legislation to match the AEC's program.

NSW Local Government election 2021

In our report we show problems with many more electorates due to iVote than the NSW electoral commission acknowledges. A companion web site is here.
